Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of text that the websites you visit send to your browser. Allow information for websites to remember about your visit, which can make it easier for you to visit the sites again and make the sites more useful to you. "A restriction of cookies is that they can only be read by a web page from the same domain that set them"
